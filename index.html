fml.network

What

fml.network is a VPN set up by a few people from planet earth. We don't want to hide our identities, or change our digital location - we are just exploring networking and learning about IPv6 routing as we go.

Who

  • Tom Wiesing is a PhD candidate in the KWARC Group at FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg
  • Leonhard Kuboschek consults with financial service providers during the day

Why

Centralization has encircled most common internet applications - sharing files, thoughts and ideas is increasingly performed via large service providers with commercial interests. We aim to provide a platform to facilitate exchange of ideas and thoughts without the middlemen.

At the same time, building our own routed VPN has provided us with ample insights into the workings of networking technology - far beyond the theoretical knowledge from Computer Networks classes.
